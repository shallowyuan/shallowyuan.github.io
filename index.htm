Zehuan Yuan

I'm a third year PhD student advised by Professor LU Tong in Department of computer science and technology at Nanjing University. Before that, I received my B.S and M.S degree from Department of computer science and technology, Nanjing University in 2012 and 2014, respectively. From 2015/10-2017/03, I was a visiting student at the University of Michigan , under the supervision of Professor Jia Deng .

My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. Particularly, I'm interested in unsupervised object discovery, image co-segmentation and action understanding in videos.
